Understanding SGPA and CGPA

SGPA and CGPA are essential metrics used in academia to evaluate a student's performance. While SGPA measures performance in a specific semester, CGPA provides an overall measure across multiple semesters.

Why is CGPA Important?

CGPA plays a crucial role in shaping a student's academic and professional future. Many universities and employers consider CGPA as a standard for admissions, scholarships, and job eligibility. A consistent CGPA reflects dedication and consistent performance.

How to Calculate CGPA from SGPA?

The process to convert SGPA to CGPA is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Add up the SGPA of all completed semesters.
  • Divide the total by the number of semesters.
  • The result is your CGPA.
